History of ramen

Origin

According to historians, the more plausible theory is that ramen was introduced to Japan in the late 19th or early 20th centuries by Chinese immigrants living in Yokohama Chinatown. According to the records of the Yokohama Ramen Museum, ramen originated in China and made its way to Japan in 1859.
